AI Fundamentals for Legal Professionals

Understanding the AI Landscape

  • Distinguishing genuine AI from sophisticated automation
  • Many "AI companies" are actually automation companies with clever marketing
  • Critical distinction: Automation vs. Artificial Intelligence

Key Insight: LLMs as Prediction Engines

Large Language Models (LLMs) aren't true artificial intelligence—they're sophisticated prediction engines that excel at pattern recognition and text generation based on training data. Understanding this distinction is crucial for setting appropriate expectations and use cases in legal practice.
